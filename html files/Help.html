Account & Profile Settings

How to Update your Profile :-

            To update your profile ,move your cursor to your Settings and go to upadate profile then click choose files.

            It goes to your own files then you can select your profile picture.

How to Change your User Name :-

            To change user name ,move your cursor to your Settings and go to change user name.

            There you can change your user name and add some line about yourself in description box also.
