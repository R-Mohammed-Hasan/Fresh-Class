Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)









Does Freshclass help me get a job?

      Freshclass is a training & skilling program that gives students in-classroom training & a guaranteed internship at the end of the program so students build their profile & become ready for placement opportunities. Freshclass will arrange for placement opportunities at the end of the program within Freshworks & outside showcasing the proof-of-work done during the classroom training & during internships.

What is expected of me during the Freshclass programme?

      Once students clear the admission process & become part of the Software Academy program, we expect them to maintain a high degree of commitment, motivation and hard work, throughout their association with FWSA. We evaluate the students through attendance % and monthly performances. If a student lacks attendance/performance consistently for 3 months in a row, in spite of multiple interventions & communication, they might not be allowed to continue with the Freshclass program.

What is the duration of the program?

       12 months in-classroom practical training program with a monthly stipend 6-12 months of internship experience with monthly salary

Will I receive a degree/certificate when I graduate from Freshclass?

       Not a degree, but a certificate of course completion & internship experience will be provided followed by a job interview at Freshworks & partnering organizations.
